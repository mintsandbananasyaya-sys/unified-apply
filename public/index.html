What is this event?

Unified Events Season 1 is a 200-player Minecraft civilization event set in Europe. Players will form civilizations, build alliances, engage in diplomacy, and compete across a full season. This is primarily a roleplay and lore-driven experience — not a PvP-focused server.

Before you apply

Applications are reviewed by staff within 24–72 hours. You have a maximum of 2 attempts per season. If your first application is denied, staff may grant you a second attempt. A second denial means you cannot apply again until a new season opens.
